Legend of the team

Lionel Andreas Messi

Messi is the greatest player in the history of Barcelona and football. Through which he achieved many individual and group awards

Messi was born in 1987 in the Argentine city of Rosario. He joined FC Barcelona in 2004 at the Lamcia Academy and then joined the first team in 2006. He left the club in 2021 after 17 years full of successes and achievements, until he became a club legend until the present time, hopefully. To return to the club one day. Messi has won many individual and group awards at the local and European levels. He cannot be compared to any player in the history of football.He moved to Paris Saint-Germain after his departure, where he spent two years under the booing and hatred of the fans, and then he was able to achieve his dream and the dream of his fans by achieving the World Cup, which is the greatest tournament in history, so he sat on the throne of football and moved to Inter Miami to end his career.
